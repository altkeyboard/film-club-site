## Submission basics
- **Recommended length:** 30 seconds to 5 minutes
- **File types:** MP4 preferred (or share a link)
- **Credits:** Use first names only (or initials)

## How to submit
Submit using the club’s approved method:
- (Paste your teacher/advisor email)  
or  
- (Paste a school-approved form link)

## What to include
- Film title
- Runtime
- Format (animation/live action/etc.)
- A 1–2 sentence description
- Anything you want feedback on (sound, pacing, story clarity, etc.)

## Privacy reminder
Do not include last names, personal contact info, or private addresses in film credits or descriptions.
